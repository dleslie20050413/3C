Navigating and Querying the Habitable Zone map

Right-Click brings up a form that lets you change the total area on display. You can change the # of pixels the map uses You can change the size of the rows/columns in tiles You can change the upper left tile (the "starting" tile)

Alt-Right-Click zooms in to display less map and larger tiles (unless the zoom is already maxed in)

Ctrl-Right-Click zooms out to display more of the map and smaller tiles (unless the zoom is already maxed out)

Left-Click: Displays the XY Navigation form
Filling out the X and Y coords and pressing Go displays the map with those Coords at the center
pressing dismiss makes the form go away

Ctrl-Left-Click:Displays Town Navigation Form
Filling in the name of a town and pressing Go displays the map with the town at the top center
Pressing dismiss makes the form go away

Alt-Left-Click (on a town tile) will display the tile history form. Non town tiles will rarely, if ever, have a history tidbit.
If you hover over the form a history tidbit for the tile, if any, will display.
This is mostly useful for inhabited tiles.
Click dismiss to make the form go away

Shift-Left-Click
Lets you choose between post transit and pre transit displays.
Pre transit is only good to see what used to be under water. dismiss makes the form go away

Ctrl-Shift-Left-Click displays dryness and slope information

Mouse Wheel up moves north

Right Arrow key moves east

Left Arrow key moves west

up arrow moves north

down arrow moves south

space bar or page down moves a screen down

shift space or page up moves a screen up

end moves all the way south

home moves all the way north

I don't yet have a hot key way to move "all east" or "all west". But mouse click to get the XY navigation form: X of 0 is "all west" an X of 200 is "all east". <--The little triangles are slopes The apex of the little tiangles shows the high end of a slope The base of the little triangle show the low end of a slope-->
